Graduate Admission

Application Process

  • Complete the online application form
  • Submit $100 non-refundable application fee
  • Provide a one-page statement of practice, influences, and goals
  • Submit three recommendation letters
  • Include undergraduate transcripts
  • Upload portfolio demonstrating technical and conceptual skills
  • Optional: additional materials like essays or work in other media

Admission Timeline

  • Preliminary notifications: early February
  • Final admission offers: early March
  • Enrollment decision deadline: April 15

Finances, Fees, and Tuition

  • Total estimated cost 2025–2026: $77,192
  • Tuition: $48,500
  • Health Insurance: $3,422
  • Housing and Meals: $17,750
  • Course Materials and Supplies: $5,796
  • Personal Expenses: $1,258
  • Transportation: $466
