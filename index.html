Welcome to Yale School of Art

Introduction

The Yale School of Art, part of Yale University, offers an intensive, critically rigorous environment for artists and designers to explore, challenge, and expand their creative practice.

The School provides graduate-level education through MFA programs in Graphic Design, Painting/Printmaking, Photography, and Sculpture. The academic culture emphasizes independent studio work, critical dialogue, and intellectual engagement.
