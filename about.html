About Yale School of Art

MFA Degrees Overview

The Yale School of Art offers MFA degrees to a highly selective cohort, cultivating exceptional artistic talent and conceptual rigor.

Academic Environment

Students develop independent studio practice, supported by courses on drawing, color theory, video and film, and interdisciplinary electives, alongside critiques and workshops.

Thesis Portfolio Development

Graduate students focus on creating a cohesive portfolio demonstrating mastery and engagement with broader artistic questions.

Public Programming

Yale School of Art organizes exhibitions, lectures, and workshops that connect students with a broad network of artists, curators, and professionals.
